About Bean & Bear Café

Founded in 2023 by two Edinboro University and General McLane graduates, Bean & Bear Café is Edinboro PA's local home for coffee and espresso. We only offer the best fresh made products, always making from scratch rather than selling premade food. ​ Named after our beloved cat Muenster "Bear" and our dog-in-law Fletcher "Bean", we know the importance of the "Bean" or "Bear" in everyone's life! ​ Stop in for baked goods, breakfast sandwiches, and lunch menu options! We provide free Wi-Fi for our customers in a chill environment, making our café the best place in town to study, do homework, and hang out.
